The critical insight: **No centralized "Halal Creative Market" exists.** The space is fragmented across Etsy, Envato, CodeCanyon, RawPixel, and Gumroad — with no compliance guarantee.

### Platforms for Selling Digital Products

| Platform | Best For | Fee | Automation Level |
|----------|---------|-----|-----------------|
| **LemonSqueezy** (Primary) | Micro SaaS + Digital Products | 5% (free) → 2% ($21/mo) | Full API — merchant of record, handles VAT/global tax |

The **hybrid model** wins: Gumroad for discovery + LemonSqueezy for your own site checkout (tax compliance) + your own Next.js storefront for maximum margin and branding.
